I want to delete my account or unsubscribe the service


If you wish to delete your account or unsubscribe from our service, the process is simple. Please send an email to our support team with the subject line "unsubscribe" and include the message "I want to unsubscribe mmc-noticebot" in the body of the email. Our team will process your request promptly and ensure your subscription is canceled.
